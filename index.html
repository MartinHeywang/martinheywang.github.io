My Story

First of all, I got into programming because it is the first thing that I could scratch my head on enough. I love to solve problems, even if they can be really useless sometimes.

At school, when I was 13, we used to do some Scratch programs and this how I got into programming. Because I needed more, I started learning my first real programming language : Java. One year later, I found myself presenting a simple hangman game to the “party” of my school, I was already 15 years old.

But still, I needed more. I started a project, "PRODUCTS.", a game -, and it lasted for over a year and a half ! It was the first time I had the motivation to keep a project for more that long.

But this project got to an end as I got into web development - I needed to breath, to do something else. This is where I am currently, and I’m not excluding the fact that I may restart PRODUCTS; but probably under another nickname and probably with other technologies (Web Assembly ???) .

.. and if you wonder, I still want MORE !!!
